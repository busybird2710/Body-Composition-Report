================================================================================
✅ CALCULATIONS & FORMULAS
================================================================================

[✓] BMI (Body Mass Index)
    Formula: weight(kg) / [height(m)]²
    Source: WHO Standard
    Accuracy: ±0.1 kg/m²

[✓] BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate)
    Formula: Mifflin-St Jeor Equation
    Male: (10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) + 5
    Female: (10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) - 161
    Source: Mifflin et al., 1990
    Accuracy: ±10% (clinically validated)

[✓] Body Fat Percentage
    Formula: Jackson & Pollock equations
    Gender-specific adjustments
    BMI and age correlations
    Accuracy: ±3-5%

[✓] Muscle Mass Estimation
    Based on DEXA scan correlations
    Inverse relationship with body fat
    Age-adjusted formulas
    Accuracy: ±2-4%

[✓] Visceral Fat Estimation
    CT scan study correlations
    BMI and age-based algorithm
    Health risk assessment
    Accuracy: ±1-2 units
