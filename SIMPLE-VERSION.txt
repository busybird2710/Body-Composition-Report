🧮 FORMULAS (Verified):

BMI = weight(kg) / [height(m)]²
Source: WHO Standard

BMR (Male):
  = (10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) + 5

BMR (Female):
  = (10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) - 161

Source: Mifflin-St Jeor Equation
Accuracy: ±10% (Medical Standard)

Biological Age:
  Calculated from all biomarker deviations
  
Wellness Index:
  Score 0-100 based on target range compliance
