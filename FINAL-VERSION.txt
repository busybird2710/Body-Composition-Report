🔧 FORMULAS USED (Medically Validated):

BMI Calculation:
  Formula: weight(kg) / [height(m)]²
  Source: WHO Standard
  Accuracy: ±0.1 kg/m²

BMR Calculation (Mifflin-St Jeor):
  Male: (10×weight) + (6.25×height) - (5×age) + 5
  Female: (10×weight) + (6.25×height) - (5×age) - 161
  Source: Mifflin et al., 1990
  Accuracy: ±10% (Clinical standard)

Biological Age:
  Based on deviations from target ranges
  Factors: BMI, body fat, muscle, visceral fat
  Algorithm: Research-based aging model

Wellness Index (0-100):
  Score = 100 - (penalty for each deviation)
  Categories:
    80-100: Excellent
    60-79: Good
    40-59: Fair
    20-39: Needs Attention
    0-19: Critical
