================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING:
================================================================================

Issue: PDF button does nothing
Solution: Check browser console for errors. Ensure html2pdf.js loaded successfully.

Issue: PDF quality is poor
Solution: Already optimized with 2x scale and 98% quality. This is the best setting.

Issue: PDF is blank
Solution: Wait a few seconds after clicking. Large reports take time to render.

Issue: Download doesn't start
Solution: Check browser's download settings. May need to allow downloads.

Issue: Filename is wrong
Solution: Ensure person name is filled in the form. Filename uses this data.
