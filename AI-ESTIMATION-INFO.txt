📊 ESTIMATION FORMULAS:

FOR MALES:

Total Body Fat %:
  = (1.20 × BMI) + (0.23 × age) - 16.2
  Range: 5-50%
  
Skeletal Muscle Mass %:
  = 44 - (body_fat × 0.4) - (age × 0.08)
  Range: 20-50%
  
Trunk Subcutaneous Fat %:
  = body_fat × 0.45
  Range: 5-30%
  
Visceral Fat Level:
  = (BMI - 18) × 0.8 + (age - 20) × 0.05
  Range: 1-30

FOR FEMALES:

Total Body Fat %:
  = (1.20 × BMI) + (0.23 × age) - 5.4
  Range: 12-55%
  
Skeletal Muscle Mass %:
  = 38 - (body_fat × 0.35) - (age × 0.07)
  Range: 20-45%
  
Trunk Subcutaneous Fat %:
  = body_fat × 0.50
  Range: 10-35%
  
Visceral Fat Level:
  = (BMI - 18) × 0.6 + (age - 20) × 0.04
  Range: 1-25
